Research

My research is focused on modulation of peptide self-assembly and amyloid aggregation using molecular as well as physical perturbants. Amyloid related diseases, e.g. Alzheimer's disease, diabetes type II, Parkinson's disease etc. are major age related problems at present and affect millions of people worldwide. Due to increased life expectancy, particularly in developing countries the number of cases of dementia is predicted to triple by 2050.

Motivating my work is the clear and urgent need to develop a cost effective tretment of amyloid related disorders.
